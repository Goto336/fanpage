About

Uncharted is a third person action adventure game that has a series of six games so far, but leaving "Uncharted 4: A Thief's End" the final chapter of Nathan Drake's story. In Uncharted, you play as Nathan Drake who is a treasure hunter who goes on many adventures to find the greatest treasures out there. But, these adventures end up leading him and his friends into trouble as they have to fight against tyrants leading from mafias to vicious war generals and their army, and eventually an old associate turned psycho.

Uncharted has one of the best story telling shown in any video game. For it's not just about going out to dangerous places finding treasure and shooting people. The story gets you connected to each charater and it makes you want to keep playing to see how it all ends

Along with the story and the crazy shooting, the game also has challenging puzzles to solve inorder to get to the next area, and has a lot of climbing.

The game even features it's own multiplayer for players to go head to head against each other in game modes like "Team Deathmatch", "King of the Hill", "Bounty Hunt", "Command", "Plunder", and "Classic" (Classic game mode is only in Uncharted 4). Plus the game has "Survival" mode, which is a game mode where 3-4 players play together and survive against wave after waves of enemies eventually leading up to boss battles or as the game calls it "Warloads".

Nathan Drake

Nathan Drake, the leading protagonist himself. One of gaming funniest smartasses. He has retired of his life of treasure hunting and now with his wife Elena Fisher and his daugther Cassie. But back in those days, when shit hits the fan, Nate would be your man to have. First appearance in Uncharted 1: Drake's Fortune

Harry Flynn

Harry Flynn was an English treasure hunter and fortune seeker, an employee of the Serbian war criminal Zoran LazareviÄ‡, and a one-time associate of Nathan Drake and Chloe Frazer. Unlike Drake, Harry dies in the second installment in the seires. First appearance in Uncharted 2: Among Thieves

Chole Frazer

Chloe Frazer is an Indian-Australian treasure hunter and thief for hire; she's a skilled professional whose reputation often makes her highly demanded in the criminal underworld. She was also an associate, a major ally, and a former love interest to fellow treasure hunter Nathan Drake, and has also worked with other people such as Harry Flynn, Charlie Cutter, Nadine Ross, and Samuel Drake. First appearance in Uncharted 2: Among Thieves

Katherine Marlowe

Katherine Marlowe was the English leader of an over 400-year old Hermetic Order. Ruthless and manipulative, Marlowe was also the leader of a highly-trained and well-armed group of agents who acted as her order's enforcers. Marlowe served as a central character and the main antagonist of Uncharted 3: Drake's Deception, competing with protagonists Nathan Drake and Victor Sullivan towards finding the lost city, the Atlantis of the Sands. First appearance in Uncharted 3: Drake's Deception

Rafe Adler

Rafe Adler is an American businessman and treasure hunter who serves as the main antagonist of Uncharted 4: A Thief's End. A former associate of brothers Nathan and Sam Drake, Rafe initially allied himself with them to search for Henry Avery's lost treasure , later competing with the two after he lost trust and patience with both the brothers and wanting to claim it for himself. He also hired the services of PMC Shoreline, led by Nadine Ross. First appearance in Uncharted 4: A Thief's End
